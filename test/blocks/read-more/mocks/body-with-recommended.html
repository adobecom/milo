Adobe and MLB enlist fans to #ExpressYourFandom with Adobe Express for Opening Day

Baseball is officially back, and Adobe and Major League Baseball (MLB) are stepping up to the plate to continue to bring America’s favorite pastime to the next generation of fans with eight new baseball-inspired templates from Adobe Express.

Starting today, MLB fans can access the new curated Adobe Creative Cloud Express templates to upgrade their signage game, using fun animated graphics, their favorite team colors, and iconography to incorporate when designing.
